Muhammad Yunus


Muhammad Yunus is a Bangladeshi social entrepreneur and economist. who founded the Grameen Bank and pioneered the concepts of microcredit and microfinance. His work earned him the Nobel Peace Prize in 2006, making him the first Bangladeshi to receive this honor. Through his efforts, Yunus has impacted millions of lives by enabling people to lift themselves out of poverty.
